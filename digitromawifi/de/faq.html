How can we help you?

What is DigitRoma WiFi?

DigitRoma WiFi is a project of Roma Capitale that offers citizens, tourists and, in general, to the city users free connectivity in wi-fi mode and an integrated platform of services and contents accessible in mobility in the areas of the city served by the network of public hot spots.

Is there a limit of daily use of the service?

The service is available for 4 hours a day.

Do the 4 hours have to be continuous or are they available throughout the day?

They are counted based on the actual navigation time within 24 hours. It is therefore advisable to log out every time you decide to stop browsing. For this reason it is advisable not to close the welcome page which is accessed after authentication.

How do I register for the service?

Anyone in possession of a mobile device (notebook, smartphone or tablet) can register simply indicating name, surname, email and telephone number, if in possession of a SIM of an Italian operator. Even users who are in possession of a foreign SIM can use the registration procedure to allow the use of the service. At the end of the registration phase, the user is shown the success of the operation directly on the authentication portal.

Why is it necessary to provide the mobile number?

To identify those who access the Internet and improve system and transaction security. The number of the SIM therefore serves to avoid the request for the identity document, already acquired when the SIM is purchased from the telephone company.

Why is an email box required between the fields to be filled in?

JJust to cross check in your favor. By including an e-mail box, in fact, the password will be sent by e-mail. If the password did not arrive by mail, it could mean to say that its field is filled out incorrectly.

How do I get the credentials to use the service?

After indicating name, surname, SIM number, password and email box, the user must make a free call to validate the registration. Once the call is over, the system sends an e-mail with username and password to the e-mail box indicated in the registration procedure. It is therefore recommended not to delete the e-mail containing this data.

How to recover Username and Password?

TThe Username is the mobile phone number used for recording. If you register again with the same number, the username will consist of the mobile number used plus a sequential numeric suffix. Instead, the password can be recovered by clicking on "Reset password" on the welcome page and re-entering the SIM number through which you registered with the service. You will receive an SMS and an email with the new password for access.

How can I log out after I have logged on?

In order to disconnect from the DigitRoma WiFi service once authenticated, it is important not to close the portal page. However, if this page is mistakenly closed, you can access again by clicking to the DigitRoma WiFi website..

How to unsubscribe from the DigitRoma WiFi service?

It is possible to cancel the membership of the service by sending an email to info.digitroma@comune.roma.it or by calling the Contact Center at 060606.

What is Free Italia WiFi and how do I use it?

"Free ItaliaWiFi" is a project for building up the first National federated network of free wireless internet access. With "Free Italia WiiFi" you can browse for free not only in the public WiFi areas of your city, but also in the other WiFi networks of the Administrations that have joined the National initiative. In this way, a DigitRoma WiFi member can use on the National territory the free public network interconnected to Free Italy Wi-fi, specifying in the username the realm, an attribute that identifies its own domain. For the DigitRoma WiFi romawifi specifies the source network (eg numerotelefonico@romawifi.it). Similarly, guest users with access credentials of other public wireless networks, included in the Free Italia WiFi project, can use the free navigation on the DigitRoma WiFi network in the Municipal areas, specifying the origin of the reference network (ex. phonenumber@provinciawifi) The general purpose of "Free ItaliaWiFi" is to promote collaboration between public administrations, in order to enhance the design and creation of free wireless connectivity networks on the territories of the Administration, aimed at the federation of these territorial networks in a single National WiFi infrastructure. For more information, visit the website www.freeitaliawifi.it
